Black Jack
Lets play a game of 21,
also known as Black Jack!

200pts to win; 50pts per round.

player

dealer

  

Note:

If one sum is greater than 21, the other player automatically wins the round.

If both are over 21, no one wins the round.

If neither sum is over 21, the player with the sum closer to 21 wins the round.

If both have the same sum, it is a tie and no one wins the round.
